The mother is quite the unique depiction of a mother I haven’t quite seen in other games: She is very loving, caring, and forgiving about her children in. When Ark broke a window and Karam covered him, she was **proud** of her upstanding son, and not mad at all that they broke a window or tried to hide the truth (by shifting the blame). When Nayan spilled hot chocolate, her first reaction after panicked concern was relief that it was only chocolate and her kids are fine.
>
>Unlike in other games where loving parents are explicitly said as such, leaving loving messages and caring in a simple way about their child (it is fine to make us understand them as loving parents characters); Karaneko’s mother is shown caring in a very *real* way, where nothing matters more to her than their growth as a remarkable person and their safety. And that concern is in effect only with positive actions, kindness and love, no trace of scolding or anger.
